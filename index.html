SPegetti Slammer

A 10 lb sledgehammer. A city. Chaos.

Controls

  • W A S D — Move (opposite keys cancel)
  • Left Shift (hold) — Run
  • Mouse — Look around (first person)
  • Left Click — Swing sledgehammer (3.5s cooldown)
  • Esc — Release mouse

How It Works

  • Hit a victim with the hammer head — they become strands of spaghetti.
  • Hit them with the handle — they just get bumped.
  • Swings alternate: first swing right, next swing left, repeat.
  • Watch out for the cars — they don't stop.
